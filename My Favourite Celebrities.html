My Favourite Celebrities


Shila Amzah

Shila Amzah or her real name is NurShahila binti Amir Amzah is a Malaysian singer-songwriter. In the year of 2005, Shila Amzah released her second pop album called "Sha-Hila". Her single "Patah Seribu" received Pop Song of the Year and Song of the Year in the 19th Malaysian Music Awards. She continued her singing career by breaking out to Shanghai, China and eventually won the Asian Wave. As a result, Shila Amzah won the Kembara Award (International Music Breakthrough Award) from Malaysian Music Awards making She is the youngest Kembara Award honoree. Afterwards, Shila Amzah continued her singing career in mandopop industry by breaking out to Hong Kong in 2015 by releasing some new singles, doing an international press conference and establishing her second record label and global management company, Shilala (HK) Limited. Her success on international stages has gained her honorific titles such as Asia's Sweetheart, Princess of Music, and National Treasure of Malaysia.


In the year of 2013, Shila Amzah was the first Malay and Malaysian artist to be nominated at the World Music Awards after winning Asian Wave as well being the first and only Malay artist to be invited to the Global Chinese Music Awards (GCMA). She is the first and only Malay artist to have success in Chinese-speaking countries and Chinese record charts with her Sina Weibo account having more than 2.3 million followers. She was listed in the Forbes' 30 Under 30 Asia in the year of 2018, recognizing young leaders, entrepreneurs and game changers from across Asia under Entertainment and Sports category.




Noraniza Idris

Noraniza Idris or her real name is Nor Aniza binti Haji Idris born on 27 August 1968. In the Malaysian music industry, she is known as the "Queen of Ethnic Pop". The genres plays by Noraniza Idris is known as "Irama Malaysia" which is a local traditional genres with Anglo-American pop music. Lyrically, her music includes references to the revival of traditional Malay music. She began her career as a wedding singer and soon found herself in the recording studio after participating in Bintang RTM (a talent search singing contest) in 1985.
